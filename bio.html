The life and times of a number four


Did you know I was the 4th of my name? Now you do.
Born and raised in Buffalo, NY. Okay that is not entirely true. I started life out in Cheektowaga, NY where we lived until I was 6 before moving to Clarence, NY where my family still resides.
Graduate of Clarkson University in Potsdam, NY. I am a very proud Golden Knight, those four years helped define me and I am forever grateful for the bonds that were formed and the opportunities that I had there. I am a lifetime member of the fraternity that is WTSC Radio which has proudly broadcast from the Clarkson campus for over 50 years.
Moved to Worcester, MA in November of 2013. It was actually never my intention to return to Buffalo after college, but life happens, situations change and thats where I was. Buffalo will forever be home but sometimes you have to put home in the rearview mirror and branch out on new adventures.
